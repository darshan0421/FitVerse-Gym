Warm-up & Mobility Routine (10–12 minutes)

Do these before heavy lifting or intense cardio to prime muscles and reduce injury risk.

Jumping Jacks — 1 min

Full-body dynamic movement to raise heart rate.

Leg Swings — 10 each side

Loosens hip flexors and hamstrings for lower-body work.

Arm Circles — 30 sec each direction

Mobilizes shoulders before pressing movements.

Hip Hinge / Deadbug — 8–10 reps

Activates posterior chain and core stability.


Pro tip: start with bodyweight versions and progress gradually. Always control range of motion.

Safety Tips & Best Practices

  • Always warm up 8–12 minutes before every
  • Focus on proper form before increasing weight.
  • Start with light weights and progress gradually.
  • Use a spotter or safety equipment for heavy lifts.
  • Perform exercises with controlled, steady movements.
  • Stay hydrated throughout your workout.
  • Give each muscle group 48–72 hours to recover.
  • Breathe properly — exhale on effort, inhale on release.
  • Wear proper gym attire and supportive shoes.
  • Stop immediately if you feel pain or discomfort.
